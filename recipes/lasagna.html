World's Best Lasagna

Description

This lasagna recipe takes a little work, but it is so satisfying and filling that it's worth it!

Ingridients

  • Meat: You could also ground lamb if you prefer.
  • Onions and Garlic: Use starchy potatoes (like russet or Yukon Gold) for the best mashed potatoes. The mashed potatoes are also made with butter, onion, and Cheddar cheese.
  • Tomato Products: This shepherd's pie includes onions and carrots.
  • Sugar: All-purpose flour will thicken the beef gravy.
  • Spices and Seasonings Ketchup will add a little tang to the ground beef mixture.
  • Lasagna Noodles: Beef broth creates a gravy-like consistency in the middle.
  • Cheeses: Parmesan, mozzarella, and ricotta cheese make this lasagna extra decadent.
  • Egg: An egg helps bind the ricotta so it doesn't ooze out of the lasagna when you cut into it.

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
